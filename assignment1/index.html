About Me

Amanda Hoffman grew up in Irvington, NJ before moving to California to pursue a career in llama wrangling. When that didn't work out, she moved back east to study English at Rutgers University.

An interest in html was sparked in the late 1990s when she wanted to write in purple in chat rooms. When she is afk, Amanda enjoys knitting, crocheting, and star-gazing.
